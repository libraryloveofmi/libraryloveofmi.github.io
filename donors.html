Become a Donor

Support the Platform That Bridges Books to Students

Your monetary support keeps our platform free for schools and libraries—so more students get the resources they need.

Where does my donation go?

Your donation helps keep the platform running and free for schools and libraries. Funds support website hosting, maintenance, and improvements, as well as outreach to connect more educators with donors. We don’t take any portion of wishlist purchases—your support simply makes it possible for more communities to access the resources they need.

Donation options

You can support our mission in a way that works best for you. Become a monthly supporter for just $0.99 to help sustain the platform year-round, make a one-time donation of any amount, or choose a direct impact option—$5 helps keep one school active on the site for a full year, and $10 helps us add a new school, expanding access to more students and communities.

Partnerships and Businesses

Partner with us to make a bigger impact. We offer flexible opportunities for businesses, organizations, and community groups to support and grow our mission—whether through sponsorships, regional support, donation matching, or collaborative initiatives. In return, partners receive meaningful visibility through website recognition, sponsored school highlights, and social media features, all while demonstrating a clear commitment to supporting schools, libraries, and the communities they serve
