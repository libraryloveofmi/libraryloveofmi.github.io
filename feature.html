Policies

Privacy Policy

Library Love of Michigan is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with applicable laws, including those of the State of Michigan and the United States.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through our website, including our contact form. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and any message you choose to submit.

This information is used solely to respond to inquiries, communicate with you, process donation-related questions, and improve our services.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Limited information may be shared with trusted third-party service providers (such as website hosting or payment processors) only as necessary to operate our services.

By submitting your information through our contact form, you consent to being contacted by Library Love of Michigan via email or phone regarding your inquiry.

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data; however, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Donation Policy

Library Love of Michigan operates as a nonprofit initiative dedicated to supporting schools and libraries across Michigan by connecting donors directly with classroom and library wishlists.

Donations are fulfilled through third-party platforms (such as Amazon or similar retailers), where donors purchase items directly for schools. Library Love of Michigan does not take possession of donated funds or goods.

Because purchases are completed through third-party vendors, we are unable to offer refunds or exchanges. Donors should review their orders carefully before completing a purchase.

While we make reasonable efforts to review submitted wishlists, we cannot guarantee the availability, accuracy, or delivery of items once ordered through third-party services.

Users may not submit false or misleading information, including fraudulent wishlist requests or misuse of the contact form.

This website and its content are provided "as is" without warranties of any kind, either express or implied. Library Love of Michigan is not liable for any damages arising from the use of this website.

Wishlist Submission Policy

Educators and librarians submitting wishlists must ensure that all requested materials are appropriate for educational use and comply with school policies and applicable laws.

By submitting a wishlist, you confirm that you are authorized to request materials on behalf of your school or library.

Library Love of Michigan reserves the right to review, approve, deny, or remove any wishlist at its discretion.

Nonprofit Transparency

Library Love of Michigan is a nonprofit initiative focused on supporting educational communities throughout Michigan. We are committed to transparency in how wishlists are shared and fulfilled.

We do not collect or distribute donation funds directly; all contributions are made by donors through third-party platforms.
